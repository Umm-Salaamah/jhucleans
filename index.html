Deep Cleaning

Learn More

Linen Hire Cleaning

A comprehensive cleaning service for properties at the end of a rental period, ensuring they are spotless for the next tenants or to meet landlord requirements. Often includes deep cleaning tasks like carpet shampooing, appliance cleaning, and wall washing.

Midstay Shortlet Cleaning

Cleaning service provided during an ongoing stay in a short-term rental (shortlet), ensuring the property remains clean and comfortable for current guests. This typically includes dusting, vacuuming, and refreshing linens, but may vary based on guest needs and stay duration.
