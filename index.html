Frequently Asked Questions

What if my Inventory inspection fails?

If we have done an end of tenancy cleaning at your property in London and your inspection inventory fails, then please contact us and we will arrange for our specialist cleaners to re-clean the property free of charge.

What is your cancellation/Rescheduling policy?

We require at least 24 hours' notice for any cancellations or changes to cleaning and linen hire services. Late cancellations or failure to provide property access may incur a fee. This ensures we value both your time and that of our cleaners. By accepting our terms and conditions, you agree to the terms of this policy. For full details, please refer to the cancellation policy.

How do I pay for cleaning?

We accept debit and credit cards only. Payment is made on our website via a secure 3rd party provider. Funds will only be taken from your card once the job has been completed. However, please note that as soon as the booking is confirmed, the estimated amount of the job is pre-authorised on your card to be sure the payment will go through after the cleaning job is completed.

Do you offer discounts for recurring or long-term bookings?

Yes, we offer discounts for regular clients and long-term bookings. If you need ongoing cleaning or linen hire services, please contact us for a tailored package.

Do I get a refund if I am not happy with the service?

Our Cleaning Company maintains a no-refund policy. If a client is dissatisfied with the service provided, we ask that any concerns be reported to us within 24 hours of the service’s completion. Upon review and confirmation of the issue, we will promptly address and rectify the problem at no additional cost within 48 hours.

Can I set up recurring cleanings?

Absolutely! We can set up recurring cleanings based on your rental schedule, whether it's daily, weekly, or after every guest's stay.

Do I need to be present during the cleaning?

No, you don't need to be present. As long as we have access to the property, our team can clean while you're away. We can coordinate with you or your guests regarding keys or lockbox codes.

How long can we hire linens and duvets for?

Our linen hire service is available for a maximum of 10 days.
