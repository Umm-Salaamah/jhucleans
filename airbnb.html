Airbnb Cleaning

Picture your guests stepping into an immaculately clean and welcoming space, where every detail has been thoughtfully prepared. At Jhu Cleans, we specialize in delivering pristine, guest-ready homes with our professional cleaning services. Whether it's a deep clean between stays or a refresh during a mid-stay short let, we ensure your property always shines. We also offer convenient linen and towel hire, complete with all the essentials, helping you provide a five-star experience and earn that coveted Superhost status.

Tasks to be Performed

  • Dust all surfaces.
  • Sweep and mop hard floors.
  • Empty trash bins and replace liners.
  • Clean countertops and sanitize all kitchen appliances.
  • Sanitize high-touch areas.
  • Clean and disinfect the toilet, sink, shower, and bathtub.
  • Wipe down all surfaces, mirrors, and windowsills.
  • Remove used linens and towels, and replace them with fresh, clean ones.
