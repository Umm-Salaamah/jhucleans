About Us

At Jhu Cleans, we’ve earned a stellar reputation for providing outstanding cleaning services tailored to meet a diverse range of needs. Whether you're looking for end-of-tenancy cleaning, Airbnb turnover services, or mid-stay refreshes, our dedicated team delivers meticulous care and attention to detail, ensuring your space is pristine every time.

We take pride in our commitment to exceed expectations, offering top-notch service at competitive rates with no hidden costs. Our goal is to leave your property spotless and welcoming, every time.

But we don’t stop at cleaning. We understand the importance of a well-prepared space for your guests. That’s why we offer an array of additional services, including linen hire, consumable replacements, and curated toiletries. From shower gels and shampoos to paper towels and coffee, we ensure every detail is taken care of allowing your guests to feel pampered and at home from the moment they arrive.

To enhance your guest experience even further, we offer bespoke welcome packs. Choose from basic essentials to luxurious hampers with quality food and fresh flowers, tailored to make every stay memorable.

Choose Jhu Cleans for a comprehensive, hassle-free service that goes above and beyond, ensuring every detail is perfect.
