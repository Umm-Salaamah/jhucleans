Linen Hire Service

High-quality linens play a crucial role in enhancing your guests' experience. We provide luxurious bed linens with a minimum thread count of 300, and plush towels with a minimum weight of 600 GSM, ensuring both comfort and durability. Upon request, we offer a comprehensive toiletries set and can replenish consumables to keep your property fully stocked. Our Linen Hire service is available for a 10-day period, after which we will arrange for collection. If needed, we can also supply a fresh set to ensure continued comfort for your guests.

FAQs
What happens if the linens are damaged or stained?
We understand normal wear and tear, but any significant damage (such as burns, rips, or permanent stains) may result in an additional charge to cover the replacement cost.
Can I add extra items like duvets, pillows, or mattress protectors?
Absolutely! You can request additional items such as duvets, pillows, or mattress protectors at an extra cost. Simply let us know in advance.
What is the process for returning the linens?
After the 10-day hire period, we will arrange a collection of the linens. If you require an extension or fresh linens, we can provide that as well. Failure to return linens within the agreed time may incur a late fee.
How much notice do I need to give for linen hire?
We recommend placing your linen hire request at least 48 hours in advance to ensure availability and delivery. However, we may be able to accommodate last-minute requests, depending on availability.
Do you provide consumables along with linen hire?
Yes, we can also supply consumables like toiletries, toilet paper, and kitchen essentials as part of our service. This is available at an additional cost and can be arranged when placing your linen order.
Can you wash my own sheets and towels?
At Jhu Cleans Linen division, we provide hotel-grade linen for hire and do not wash customer-owned goods.
