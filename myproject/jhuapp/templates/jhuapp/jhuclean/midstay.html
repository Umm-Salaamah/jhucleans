FAQs
Do you clean outdoor spaces like patios or balconies?
Yes, we can clean outdoor areas like patios, balconies, or terraces as part of the mid-stay cleaning if requested.
What if the property is excessively dirty or messy during a mid-stay clean?
If we find the property in an excessively dirty or unkempt state, we may need more time to complete the cleaning. Additional fees may apply in such cases, and we will inform you before proceeding.
Will I receive a report or confirmation after the mid-stay cleaning?
Yes, we can provide a brief report or confirmation once the cleaning is completed, including any issues we notice during the cleaning (e.g., damages, low supplies).
Can you report any damages or missing items found during cleaning?
While we do not conduct formal inspections, our cleaners will notify you if they come across any visible damage or missing items during the cleaning.
Can I inspect the property after a mid-stay clean?
Yes, we encourage hosts or property managers to inspect the property after the clean to ensure everything meets your expectations.
Do you offer additional services, like restocking supplies or running errands?
Yes, we can restock basic supplies such as toiletries, toilet paper, and kitchen essentials if requested. We can either use the supplies you provide or purchase them at an additional cost.
