Linen Hire Cleaning Service

High-quality linens play a crucial role in enhancing your guests' experience. We provide luxurious bed linens with a minimum thread count of 300, and plush towels with a minimum weight of 600 GSM, ensuring both comfort and durability.

Upon request, we offer a comprehensive toiletries set and can replenish consumables to keep your property fully stocked. Our Linen Hire service is available for a 10-day period, after which we will arrange for collection. If needed, we can also supply a fresh set to ensure continued comfort for your guests.
